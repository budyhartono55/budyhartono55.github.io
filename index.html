About me


Sumbawa is a small town in eastern Indonesia. Maybe not everyone knows where this city is, in contrast to Bali, Surabaya, Malang, or Jakarta or other cities that are quite famous in parts of Java and Nusa Tenggara.

The small town where I was born is indeed far from the hustle and bustle, far from the technological developments that are currently developing, for me there is no limit for someone to develop, design and achieve dreams, wherever they are. wherever you are born and destined, DEPENDS ON HOW YOUR PROCESS. I really want to be dedicated to the world of education in Indonesia, looking for, sharing and applying it all for future developments, especially in the world of IT, aspiring to become one of the human beings with a master's degree. here I am the dreamer, from a small town in the eastern province of Indonesia, Sumbawa.
